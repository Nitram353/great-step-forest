Four teams. Three weeks. Millions of steps for the city's trees.

Every step our teams take raises money for Trees for Cities — the charity planting urban trees and greening cities across the UK and beyond. The team that travels the furthest wins. The trees win either way.

Want in? To join the step challenge, take these two steps:
  1. sign up to the challenge on World Walking app and choose your team — that's where your steps are tracked
  2. pledge £10 to Trees for Cities on our JustGiving page

🌱 Why we're walking

Trees for Cities is a London-founded charity transforming urban areas through tree planting, greener spaces and stronger connections with nature. Its work improves air quality, supports wildlife, tackles climate change and boosts wellbeing. Through volunteering, fundraising and planting events, businesses and communities can make a visible difference. So far, the charity has planted more than two million trees across the UK and internationally.
